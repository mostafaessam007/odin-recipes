Pizza

Pizza is a popular Italian dish consisting of a round, flat base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomatoes, cheese, and various other ingredients baked at a high temperature. The classic pizza features a thin crust with a rich tomato sauce and melted mozzarella cheese, often garnished with fresh basil.
