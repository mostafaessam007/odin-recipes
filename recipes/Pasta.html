Pasta

Spaghetti Aglio e Olio is a simple yet flavorful Italian pasta dish that highlights the beauty of minimal ingredients. It features al dente spaghetti tossed in a fragrant blend of sautéed garlic and red pepper flakes in olive oil. The dish is finished with a sprinkle of freshly chopped parsley and a generous grating of Parmesan cheese, adding a touch of color and richness. The garlic is cooked until golden, infusing the oil with a rich, savory flavor, while the red pepper flakes provide a gentle heat. This dish exemplifies the essence of Italian cooking—elegantly simple, relying on high-quality ingredients to create a satisfying meal that’s perfect for any occasion. It's quick to prepare, making it a favorite for weeknight dinners or a last-minute gathering with friends. Serve it with a side of crusty bread to soak up the delicious oil!

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Salt
  • Water

Steps

  1. Prepare the Dough
  2. Mix the Ingredients
  3. Knead the Dough
  4. Wrap and Rest
  5. Roll out the Dough
  6. Cut the Pasta
  7. Dust with Flour
  8. Cook the Pasta
  9. Drain and Serve
